# StudyDesk Setup Reference

- Title: `Set up studydesk`
- URL: [Panopto Viewer](https://usq.au.panopto.com/Panopto/Pages/Viewer.aspx?id=98a53c84-8e6f-4ee8-b362-b0b200127aee)
- Source note: extracted on 2026-04-22 from Panopto captions available through the viewer.

## Extracted Workflow

1. Start by checking the integrated shell and what is already visible to students.
2. Treat `Getting Started` and Orientation material as the first urgent clean-up area.
3. Replace or update the short orientation/introduction recording if needed.
4. Hide assessments, practice exams, and other not-ready activities first.
5. Use student view to verify what students can see after each release/visibility change.
6. Check the StudyDesk schedule settings early.
7. Make the StudyDesk schedule and `course administrator` settings agree on:
   total teaching weeks
   flexible learning period count
   any other institutional week-structure fields
8. For the shorter offering pattern shown in the recording:
   keep orientation material in Orientation Week
   combine or move content from later weeks into earlier weeks so the shell matches the shorter delivery
   rename sections where necessary so the title matches the moved content
9. Review copied content for stale references to the old week pattern.
10. Check quiz due dates and other copied deadlines after the week restructure.
11. Release Orientation content now, but do not release all later weeks immediately.
12. Send an announcement telling students to watch the course-introduction material during Orientation Week.

## Important Notes From The Recording

- The guide repeatedly says the shell should be checked from the learner side after hiding/releasing items.
- The recording treats schedule settings as high-priority because students check them early.
- When adapting a shell from a longer semester to a shorter one, the guide prefers moving/copying week blocks one by one instead of doing a blind bulk release.
