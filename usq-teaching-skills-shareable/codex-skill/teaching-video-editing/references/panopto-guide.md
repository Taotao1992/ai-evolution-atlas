# Panopto Reference

- Title: `Video editing guideline for Tutors`
- URL: [Panopto Viewer](https://usq.au.panopto.com/Panopto/Pages/Viewer.aspx?id=7e85a39e-e89c-4cfc-a5d1-ad6600838408)
- Source note: server captions were not available for this recording, so the workflow below was extracted on 2026-04-22 from the Panopto media stream using local transcription against the user's logged-in browser session.

## Extracted Workflow

1. Sign in to Panopto with the UniSQ account and open `My Folder`.
2. Create a course-specific folder before uploading tutor videos.
3. Upload the video and wait until processing finishes enough for editing controls to appear.
4. Open `Edit`.
5. In `Settings`:
   rename the video if needed
   untick the two discussion/comment options so students cannot comment under the video
   leave copyright notice optional unless requested
6. In `Captions`:
   import automatic captions
   minor recognition errors are acceptable unless a cleanup task is requested
7. In `Contents`:
   add chapter markers by dragging to the correct time and entering a title
   adjust or delete a chapter if the time/title is wrong
8. In `Cuts`:
   cut only genuinely wrong, redundant, or misleading parts
   do not cut ordinary short exercise pauses unless asked
   multiple cuts can be added, then fine-tuned by editing start/end times
9. Apply the edits and wait for Panopto to process again.
10. In `Share`:
    change visibility to the UniSQ organization scope shown in the guide
    copy the link
11. In StudyDesk:
    turn editing on
    add a `URL` activity in the correct week/section
    paste the Panopto link
    name it clearly
    move it into the right place
    make sure the StudyDesk item is not hidden

## Important Notes From The Recording

- The guide says you can share the link before all processing fully finishes, but the final verification still needs a playback check.
- The guide prefers cutting unwanted sections before doing final contents timing cleanup.
- Tutorial recordings can be linked straight into StudyDesk once the Panopto share settings are correct.
