- Source note: extracted on 2026-04-22 from Panopto captions available through the viewer.

## Extracted Workflow

1. Start in the StudyDesk gradebook and check the exam grade first.
2. If an exam allows multiple attempts, make sure the latest attempt is the one showing.
3. Export the full grade report to Excel before the PeopleSoft step.
4. Use the spreadsheet to:
   sort and scan the cohort
   check grade distribution
   look at borderline cases near pass or other decision thresholds
5. Spot-check the preview/export against the original gradebook for one or more students.
6. Investigate missing-assessment patterns before assuming a fail is correct.
7. Transfer to the institutional system only after the audit is clean.
8. In the transferred results:
   manually review result-code cases such as `IAS` and `IM`
   apply them according to the rule about exam pass/fail and assignment completion
9. Open `Examiner Return`, review the values, and approve only when the class is fully correct.
10. Repeat the approval workflow class by class.
11. Complete the grade-distribution/significance section and save.
12. The recording explicitly mentions checking whether the HD/A share is acceptable and commenting on improvement or change versus previous offerings.

## Important Notes From The Recording

- The presenter warns not to approve until everything is checked 100%.
- The recording includes real student results, so treat the workflow as confidential when sharing notes or screenshots.
- If current institutional policy differs from the recording on thresholds or result codes, current policy wins.
