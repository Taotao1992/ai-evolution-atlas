- Title: `Video editing guideline for Tutors`
- URL: [Panopto Viewer](https://usq.au.panopto.com/Panopto/Pages/Viewer.aspx?id=7e85a39e-e89c-4cfc-a5d1-ad6600838408)
- Access note: This link appears to require UniSQ/Panopto login. If the transcript or page content is unavailable, use the URL as a navigation source and follow the operational checklist below.

## Operational Checklist

1. Open the exact video and confirm course/folder ownership.
2. Record the current title, folder, and availability before editing.
3. Apply the requested edit in the least destructive way possible.
4. Check playback after the edit, including start/end points and audio.
5. Check captions if the task touches accessibility or student-facing media.
6. Confirm students can still access the intended final version.

## Clarify Before Editing

- Which video URL or folder is the correct target?
- Are you trimming content, updating metadata, or both?
- Should the original remain available as a backup?
- Are captions or transcript cleanup required?
