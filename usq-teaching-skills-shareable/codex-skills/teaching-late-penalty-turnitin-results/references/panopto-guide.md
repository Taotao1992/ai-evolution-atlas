## Operational Checklist

1. Confirm the official late-penalty rule before touching marks.
2. Gather due dates, timestamps, extensions, and exemptions.
3. Check Turnitin report availability and note exceptions.
4. Apply or prepare penalties with before/after grade values.
5. Resolve or flag special cases before release.
6. Reconfirm the correct activity and cohort before releasing results.
7. Verify the post-release state from the live system.

## Clarify Before Release

- What exact penalty formula applies?
- Which students have approved extensions or exemptions?
- Are any Turnitin issues still unresolved?
- Is the task to prepare the release, or to execute it now?
