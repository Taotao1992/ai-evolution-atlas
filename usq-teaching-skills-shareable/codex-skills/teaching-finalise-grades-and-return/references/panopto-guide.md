## Audit Checklist

1. Confirm all grading is complete.
2. Identify withheld, exempt, special-case, or manually adjusted students.
3. Check for missing grades, impossible values, and inconsistent overrides.
4. Confirm moderation or scaling steps were applied exactly once.
5. Prepare the examiner return with an audit trail of totals and exceptions.
6. Reconfirm the target course before final submission.

## Clarify Before Final Submission

- Is the request only to prepare and check, or to submit the final return?
- Which grading scale and rounding rules apply?
- Are there withheld or unresolved integrity cases?
- Does another approver need to sign off first?
